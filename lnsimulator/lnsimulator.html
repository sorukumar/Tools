Lightning Network Simulator

Explore how payments route through the Lightning Network. Create networks, send payments, and watch them find their way!

How to use:

  1. Choose the number of nodes and channel probability, then click "Regenerate Network"
  2. Select a sender node, receiver node, and payment amount
  3. Click "Send Payment" to see the payment route through the network
